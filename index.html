About

I’m Anoushka Bhardwaj, a senior Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Massachusetts Amherst (Class of 2026) with a focus on manufacturing, product design, and sustainable engineering. Outside of engineering, I’m part of the UMass Badminton Team and enjoy exploring 20th-century Japanese literature.
